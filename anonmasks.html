Anonymous Masks

given the unprecedented situation people found themselves in with Covid - 19 and lockdown, people needed to find new and different ways to occupy themselves.

Masks recommended now and compulsory in some situations. Having to wait too long for orders to come through so to ensure I had a mask to wear - made my own. Like many other people (who have made PPE, masks etc) you help where you can , so ended up making masks for family and friends. Anonymous
