JOE DALTON

I am primarily a landscape painter however my background is in film set design and during lockdown I started combining my love for painting, the outdoors, filmmaking and cycling into some videos of me heading out into my local landscape on my bike in search of  views then finishing with me creating a painting of a part of my ride! It has been a great little exercise for me in creativity and a way to showcase my practise.
